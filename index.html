work experience

i'm currently interning at a Thai startup called Guestasy for the past 7 months. i can best describe my position as a... software engineer? Here are some of the projects i've worked on at the company:

  1. Report Generator
  2. Email Notifier for unpublished review responses

now, let's talk about my magnum opus at the company. The report generator. Our company offers reputation management services to various hotels in the South-East Asia region. Some services we include are:

  1. Responding to Reviews - We respond to reviews left by customers on our client's review pages in multiple langauges
  2. Monthly Reports - Every month, We send out a report to our clients detailing their performance based on various metrics such as review count, Languages, etc.

but, sending out reports every month was a very tedious task. You had to go to mongodb, query all the required numbers, Input all the reviews into an LLM for other analysis and finally manually put them in in canva.

so I automated this whole system by writing a python project to do all of this. It fetches records from MongoDB, Processes them, and puts it into a PPT, Converts it to PDF, and the uploads it to an S3 bucket where we can send them to our clients. there's also various other features such as AI analysis of the reviews which i worked on.

it's a really fun project to be working on and I learned a lot about the various libraries in python such as openai, pymongo, and many more. New features, Maintenance and optimisations are always being worked on. The whole project is 4k+ lines as of now.

personal projects

my main project so far is rankPredictor. it's a website which allows you to predict your rank for various entrance exams by inputting your marks. it currently has data for MET 2025 & 2024, KCET 2024. i had made it with a friend while anxiously waiting for my MET result.

you can check it out at https://rankpredictor.in, here's how it looks:

it works on the basis of linear interpolation. data is scraped from the web and and college campuses for various places, and the user's score is interpolated based on the scraped data to return a rank. it utilises simple HTML, CSS and Javascript with no bloatware frameworks like react. for MET 2024, it was very accurate for ranks greater than 7k with only a 1k difference in predicted and actual rank.
